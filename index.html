What is the AU Bang?

The AU Bang is a Fire Emblem: Three Houses Big Bang focused on alternate universe works! Writers work on an alternate universe fic, and each artist is paired with a fic to create fanart for.

The AU Bang welcomes all AU concepts, from coffee shop AUs to high seas AUs to space exploration AUs. We also welcome ship content, gen content, and everything from fluff to angst - the only requirement is it takes place in an alternate universe setting. NSFW content is also allowed, although we will require all prompts for planned NSFW works to be tagged as such.

What is an AU?

From fanlore - "fanworks which change one or more elements of the source work's canon. Broadly, an AU may transplant a given source work's characters to a radically different setting, shift the genre in which their adventures occur, and/or alter one or more of their professions, goals, or backstories."

A Small History of Big Bangs

The Big Bang originated in the Drarry fandom in 2005! The titular "Big Bang" referred to the bang of their passion, but the term has since been adopted by countless fandoms to refer to any event - often, but not always, with a large required word count - in which artists and writers collaborate on prompts. We're excited to run another variation with the AU Bang, in which you're encouraged to explore the universe of possible fanwork settings.
