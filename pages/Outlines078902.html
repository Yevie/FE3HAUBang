AU Setting: Space Opera

Content Warnings: Past/Implied Sexual Assault/Rape, Unplanned Pregnancy

Major Characters: Non-binary Byleth, Claude, Dimitri, Edelgard (male genderbend), Kamui and Mikoto (Byleth's children), mentioned Felix and Sylvain, Glenn (son of Sylvix, friends with Mikoto and Kamui), Celica (daughter of Sylvix)

Ship(s): Past Byleth/Edelgard, implied Byleth/Claude/Dimitri, implied Felix/Sylvain, implied Glenn/Kamui (children ship), implied Celica/Mikoto (children ship)

Summary: Byleth is the disgraced commander of the Galactic Alliance of Humankind's Human Army, known galaxy wide for desserting the Human Army in a climactic battle against the Adrestian Empire. After killing Grand Emperor Edelgard with his own hands, he lives quietly with his twin son and daughter as a jack-of-all-trades, master of none. His most loyal commanders under him, Dimitri and Claude, find their commander, but once they do, nothing is as it seems, and eventually, Byleth's children must take on the reins of savior of the galaxy against Grima.

1. Flashback to Byleth, Imperial Admiral, killing Edelgard and deserting the battlefield.

2. Seven years later, introducing Byleth’s twins, Kamui and Mikoto. They live peacefully until Dimitri and Claude arrive to ask for help against the Shadows that Lurk in the Dark (SLD).

3. Byleth explains to the lords that they deserted the army to protect their children. Shortly before Edelgard left the Academy, he raped Byleth to cripple the Alliance, and Byleth became pregnant afterwards. They killed Edelgard during the pregnancy. Mentions of Claude and Dimitri’s own affection for Byleth.

4. Byleth explains that if Claude and Dimitri want their help, they need to take care of their children. The two agree, and Byleth tells their children to stay with the two commanders. Kamui goes with Dimitri, and Mikoto with Claude. Byleth returns to the Officer’s Academy and to war.

5. After fighting against the SLD, Byleth is heavily injured against Nemesis, but fatally wounds him and the Shadows are defeated. However, Byleth becomes comatose.

6. Ten year later, Byleth is still comatose, but is guarded by Claude. Kamui and Mikoto have grown up under their foster fathers as excellent officers. But, there are rumors of the revival of Grima. Mentions of their affections with Sylvain and Felix’s own children.

7. The two siblings investigate the rumors and learn that Byleth is the reincarnation of Sothis. Dark forces will use Byleth’s body to be Grima’s host and destroy the galaxy. To defeat Grima, they learn of the Falchion and the Sword of the Creator.

8. The siblings and their fathers obtain divine weapons to prepare to fight Grima.

9. In the battle against Grima, the siblings defeat the corrupted dragon god, but are given a choice. If they kill it with the weapons only, Grima will be revived later. But, if one of them becomes Grima’s host, that sibling must strike the final blow and die. Kamui becomes Grima’s host and kills Grima with the SotC, disappearing afterwards. Mikoto mourns her brother, but the Falchion tells her that her brother will be safe.

10. Later on, Kamui is revived from the Falchion’s powers. With Grima gone, Byleth can be awoken from their slumber. They were sleeping to seal away Grima’s power with Sothis’s help. Byleth reunites with the lords and their children are reunited.

SFW#5

AU Setting: sentai/tokusatsu inspired AU

Content Warnings: mild sparking violence, kaiju go boom!

Major Characters: Edelgard, Caspar, Hubert, Ferdinand, Rhea, Flayn, Leonie, Petra, Dimitri, Byleth, Claude

Ship(s): Gen

Summary: In the modern day, Rhea is a floating head in a tube, with Edelgard being thrown to the moon in a 'dumpster'. Five teenagers with attitude are recruited so as to stop the Emperor and her divine beasts that grow to monstrous sizes.

Millions of years ago, a great war took place on the planet. Human-looking creatures took their battle across the planet, started when the forces of Planet Adrestia murdered Queen Sothis of Garreg Mach-1. Her daughter Rhea and her niece Flayn were left to fight for her honor, but the forces of Edel Repulsvelg were growing to be too much for them to handle. With no other choice, the archbishop lured Edel into a trap, forcing her into a 'dumpster' that would be thrown onto the moon with the last of Rhea's strength.

Flayn preserved Rhea's body in a giant tube that projected her face as a giant floating head, the only means of life sustainment for the archbishop. Thanks to their dragon genes, they were able to survive for millions of years, hoping that when the day arrived and Edel was somehow freed from her prison. While they were hopeful the day would never come, their own experiences with space and seeing advancements in technology told them that it was more likely than they wished.

Over time, Rhea and Flayn kept a close eye on the mightiest that Earth had to muster, wishing to use them for the small army required to fend off Edel's presence. But they tended to see some key requirements in these forces needed for a well-oiled team; the experience that the adults may have in combat were useful, but there was still a clash of egos among them. Thus, a team of teenagers with tight friendship among them would work best.

As Rhea and Flayn disputed the final group of teens, they kept a close eye on the moon, for they knew the Day of the Dumpster would soon arrive, and the planet would be under fire from Edel Repulsvelg once more.
