SFW#1

AU Setting: Modern Day

Content Warnings: Discussions of Mental Illness, PTSD

Major Characters: Dimitri, Felix, Sylvain, Dedue, Byleth, Glenn

Ship(s): Dimitri/Felix, minor Ferdinand/Hubert, minor Byleth/Edelgard, past Byleth/Dimitri

Summary: It's not everyday you wake up and find out that you and your boyfriend not only knew you each other in a past life, but that you hated him for causing your brother's death. Felix thinks some things are better left in the past.

Part I:

-Felix wakes up, realizes that he and Dimitri both remember their past life

-They try to distinguish themselves between their past and present

-Felix freaks out over Glenn’s death

-They argue, have kind of a fight

-Felix storms out and heads to Glenn’s house

-Glenn and Felix have a discussion about the whole thing

Part II:

-Felix returns, Sylvain and Ingrid are there; him and Dimitri are awkward and don’t rlly talk, but are ok; felix just needs space

-The next day on campus, The Blue Lions see members of the Black Eagles which they killed and are now friends w, ferdibert; some awkwardness, a lot of side of characters here - it is noted that no one knows where edelgard is

-And then, they see Byleth at an inter-collegiate fencing tournament - Byleth meets the rest of the blue lions and it’s big tea because Dimitri and Byleth used to be married with heirs and all that

-Felix and Dimitri go home, Felix is jealous and Dimitri shaken

-Dimitri wants to go meet Byleth, They fight about, Dimitri points out that she’s the mother of his child, Felix is like “Go see your wife then for I care”

Part III:

-Felix camps out at Sylvain’s for a bit, best friend bonding

-Edelgard shows up, they’re like wtf, she’s like hey im Byleth’s girlfriend

-Edelgard talks about how she thinks they should allow Byleth and Dimitri to meet, and for they themselves to be there as well, the past doesn’t matter it’s all about the future etc etc

-The meeting ends up happening, it’s pretty chill, Dimitri and Byleth find out that they may still potentially have a living descendent

-Byleth and Edelgard part ways from the rest of them, they live far away and have a v different life

-Felix and Dimitri resolution and happy ending

SFW#2

AU Setting: Fodlan but Les Miserables!

Content Warnings: Violence, Character Death, Suicide Ment

Major Characters: Ashe, Caspar, Cyril, Leonie, Hilda, Flayn, Lonato, Christophe

Ship(s): Ashe/Cyril, Vague Ashe/Caspar, Minor Hilda/Leonie

Summary: After being released from prison for stealing a loaf of bread, Ashe Duran finds life as an ex-convict difficult; however, following the generous act of Flayn, a holy woman, he sets out to become an honorable man and breaks parole. Five years later and under a different name, he has made a living as a restaurateur alongside his trusted friend, Cyril, all while political tensions rise and his former parole officer Caspar rediscovers his trail. A Les Miserables inspired AU.

I: Intro. Three scenes, weeks apart. One: Prison yard. Ashe is paroled by Caspar. Two: Lonato’s home. Ashe realizes his convict past hurts his family & leaves. Three: Towns away in a church. Ashe steals & is caught by a townsperson. They bring him to Flayn, who lets him go with the valuables.

II: Timeskip. Two scenes. One: The restaurant Ashe owns with Cyril, who he befriended over the timeskip. A group of radicals led by Leonie discuss revolution. Two: After closing. Cyril is approached by Caspar, now a police inspector. While talking, Cyril mentions Ashe by name.

III: Next day. Two scenes. One: Caspar’s room. Backstory exposition while he’s getting ready. Upon exiting his room, he learns that the revolt has started. Two: Restaurant. Ashe & Cyril talk, opening for the day. Cyril mentions his meeting with Caspar; Ashe panics. Cyril helps him pack. He leaves.

IV: Two scenes. One: Ashe walks the streets. Leonie tells him about the revolt. He decides to join. Two: Ashe arrives at the barricades & talks with Leonie. She reassures him. They’re informed that a policeman (who turns out to be Caspar) was captured.

V: One scene. The group decides Ashe will execute Caspar. The two exit. Everyone disperses, leaving Leonie & Hilda, who talk. Leonie tells Hilda the revolution will fail.

VI: Two scenes. One: Alleyway with Caspar & Ashe. Ashe unties Caspar. After arguing, Caspar escapes. Two: Ashe returns to Hilda writing a letter. She repeats what Leonie told her. Ashe encourages her to take care of her regrets & not lose hope. She leaves.

VII: Two scenes. One: Caspar walks the streets. Ultimately decides to find Ashe again. Two: Leonie approaches Ashe. They argue. She asks him to make sure Hilda survives and exits.

VIII: One scene. The battle begins. Caspar dies taking a bullet for Ashe. Hilda is also shot, but not killed. Ashe carries her to the restaurant and collapses as Cyril opens the door.

IX: Epilogue. One scene. Ashe in bed with Cyril. Gives plot updates & conclusion.
