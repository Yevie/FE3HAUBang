A small town high school AU for the Golden Deer house set in 1980, inspired by the TV show ‘Freaks and Geeks’, featuring all 8 students over the course of several chapters. Studded with classic tropes such as sports events, teenage rebellion, and the mysterious games of Dungeons and Dragons. Meet the 'geeks'- Ignatz, Raphael, Lysithea, and Lorenz, and the 'freaks'- Claude, Marianne, Hilda, and Leonie, who all overcome their misconceptions of each other and realize that they have one thing in common- friendship!
