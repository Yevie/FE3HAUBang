Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

Sylvain and Felix, childhood friends, have loved one another since as long as they can remember. But feelings are complicated and both are terrified of losing their longstanding friendship. This results in a five year long drift of their friendship, leading into Felix’s sophomore year of college and Sylvain’s senior year. Different friend groups have led them to remain apart, yet it’s all the more obvious to everyone around them how much they care and how stupidly stubborn they are. Fueled by The Ashen Wolves fraternity’s annual Halloween party, Felix and Sylvain’s friends push them together through bets, careful planning and a little Halloween spirit. Will Sylvain and Felix finally have an adult conversation? For their friends sanity, they better.
