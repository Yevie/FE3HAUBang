Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

Linhardt is a memory witch — he extracts people's memories and freezes them in Crest necklaces to be revisited whenever by those who needs it. He has no memories of his life, however, no sense of time or space but the present. Byleth stumbles into his store one day, and Linhardt starts to remember.
