Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

Continuation of a Dragon Age AU, in which Ferdinand is a Templar and Hubert is a blood mage. After they were separated and forced to flee the Circle for their lives, Ferdinand and Hubert rediscover each other on the road and agree to travel together, all while Ferdinand deals with lyrium withdrawal and comes to terms with the fact that blood magic isn't as awful as it seems. Featuring lots of mutual pining. (And maybe they'll get to join the Inquisition and fight for mage rights and save the world and fall in love. You know how it goes.)
