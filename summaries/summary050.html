They say if you enter the Goddess Tower on a rainy midnight with a pocket mirror in hand, your soulmate will reveal themselves to you. But when Dimitri accompanies Felix and Annette to investigate the rumors, the three of them discover a mysterious world with strange monsters. When Dimitri realizes this world could be the missing link he needed to find the culprit behind his family's murders, he and the other Blue Lions embark on a quest to reach out to the truth.
