Summary037

Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

After the Tragedy of Duscur, Felix believes that Dimitri is like an entirely different person. He’s become violent, cruel, and completely unlike himself. Felix is validated when Dimitri reveals himself not to be the person Felix grew up with, but an imposter belonging to a group called the Agarthans. “Dimitri” forces Felix to cooperate by telling him that Dimitri is still alive, but in Agarthan captivity. When “Dimitri” drags him to Garreg Mach, he meets a member of the Golden Deer who’s eerily familiar. Felix grows close to him and learns that the white-haired young man is the real Dimitri, who had been kidnapped during the Tragedy and experimented on by Agarthans. Now that they’ve been reunited, they need to stop the imposter from taking the throne of Faerghus.
