Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

Felix is a high school student with a secret: he works part-time at a maid cafe. Since starting his job, he has had no problems keeping his schoolmates from finding out, but when transfer student Claude von Riegan shows up at the cafe one afternoon, Felix's secret is threatened. At first, Claude only seems interested in teasing Felix, but as they get to know each other, real feelings begin to develop.
