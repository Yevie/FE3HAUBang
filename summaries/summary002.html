Posted on Sat 22 August 2020 in Summaries

After being released from prison for stealing a loaf of bread, Ashe Duran finds life as an ex-convict difficult; however, following the generous act of Flayn, a holy woman, he sets out to become an honorable man and breaks parole. Five years later and under a different name, he has made a living as a restaurateur alongside his trusted friend, Cyril, all while political tensions rise and his former parole officer Caspar rediscovers his trail. A Les Miserables inspired AU.
