A young prodigee and author by the name of Lysithea, who writes under the alias of J.J. Green, was given powers by an evil witch in return for several years of her life. Lysithea's undercover fame blossomed, but at a great cost. However, everything changes when she encounters a friendly vampire that promises to show her a better life.
