A Western/Red Dead Redemption AU where each house is a gang in the Old West/Old West Fódlan. Dimitri was born into old money and a big business family but lost it all after the Tragedy of Duscur, becoming a gang leader with his childhood friends Felix, Ingrid and Sylvain. Many years later, he and his gang rob a train and he comes face to face with the new heiress of the Adrestian Company Empire, Edelgard von Hresvelg, his stepsister and the one he blames for his family's death.
