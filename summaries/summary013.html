While visiting their father in the city of Garreg Mach, a series of strange events leads Byleth to befriend a mysterious girl named Sothis and a group of super-powered students calling themselves the Ashen Wolves. When tragedy strikes Byleth’s family, the six friends set out for revenge — along the way, they’ll uncover the truth behind the many mysteries that lurk in their city.
